# Welcome to the Supervisor Blog

Welcome! This is the new home for news and writing from the team behind Supervisor.

Supervisor is an AI moderation platform that reads intent, not just keywords. It protects Discord servers, apps, and platforms with context-aware analysis, implicit-harm detection, and image moderation, in over 100 languages. If you are new here, the best places to start are the [live demo](https://supervisor.gg/demo) and the [documentation](https://supervisor.gg/docs).

## What to expect here

Articles are coming soon. We plan to write about:

- Product updates and new features as they ship
- Deep dives into how AI moderation works under the hood
- Practical guides for keeping communities healthy
- Lessons from building and scaling Supervisor

## Stay in the loop

While you wait for the first articles, you can [join our Discord](https://discord.supervisor.gg) to follow development day to day, or [get started for free](https://supervisor.gg) and see Supervisor in action on your own community.

Thanks for reading, and welcome aboard.
